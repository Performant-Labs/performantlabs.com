## Frequently Asked Qestions

[What is Salesforce Testing and why is it important?](#collapse-14514dc69eeb06ca862b)

Salesforce testing ensures that your CRM’s configurations, custom workflows, and integrations work as expected after every release. At Qeagle, we validate functionality, performance, and data integrity to maintain a seamless user experience across Salesforce Clouds.

[What types of Salesforce applications do you test?](#collapse-35c8fe469eeb06ca862b)

We test across **Sales Cloud**, **Service Cloud**, **CPQ**, **Marketing Cloud**, and **custom Salesforce apps** — covering both standard and Lightning components.

[What tools and frameworks do you use for Salesforce testing?](#collapse-c29457c69eeb06ca862b)

We use a mix of **Provar**, **Tricentis Tosca**, **Selenium**, **TestComplete**, **Postman**, **JMeter**, **TestRail**, **Zephyr**, **BrowserStack**, and **Appium**. Our test accelerators integrate seamlessly with Salesforce and major CI/CD pipelines like **Azure DevOps** and **Jenkins**.

[How do you handle test automation for Salesforce?](#collapse-d10b38769eeb06ca862b)

We build reusable automation frameworks that support Salesforce Lightning, APIs, and integrations. Automation helps us reduce regression time, increase coverage, and accelerate releases.

[Do you test Salesforce integrations with other enterprise systems?](#collapse-4cc321769eeb06ca862b)

Yes. We perform **end-to-end integration testing** with ERP, API, and third-party tools like SAP, Oracle, and ServiceNow to ensure secure and reliable data flow across systems.

[Can you test mobile and browser compatibility for Salesforce apps?](#collapse-d0cc6eb69eeb06ca862b)

Yes. With **BrowserStack** and **Appium**, we ensure consistent performance and UI behavior across devices, OS versions, and browsers.

[What makes Qeagle different in Salesforce testing?](#collapse-463e35669eeb06ca862b)

Our strength lies in combining **domain expertise, intelligent frameworks, and AI-ready automation** to help enterprises achieve **faster Salesforce releases with zero quality compromise**.
