## When Should You Invest in Performance Testing?

* Launching or scaling a customer-facing application
* Preparing for peak traffic events like sales or campaigns
* Detecting bottlenecks in high-load environments
* Meeting SLAs, compliance, and uptime guarantees
* Ensuring backend systems scale as your business grows

## Frequently Asked Qestions

[What is the goal of performance testing?](#collapse-14514dc69eeb03e55f47)

Performance testing evaluates how well your application performs under expected and peak user loads. It helps ensure that the system remains stable, responsive, and reliable—especially during high traffic or business-critical events.

[When should a business consider performance testing?](#collapse-35c8fe469eeb03e55f47)

You should consider performance testing before launching new products, during major updates, or when user traffic is expected to surge. It’s also essential for meeting compliance, SLAs, or business continuity goals.

[How does Qeagle approach performance testing?](#collapse-c29457c69eeb03e55f47)

At Qeagle, we combine tool expertise, real-user simulations, and deep diagnostics to deliver actionable insights. Our team builds performance pipelines that integrate into your CI/CD workflows and align with your product KPIs.
