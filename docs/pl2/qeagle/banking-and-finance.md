## Regulatory Compliance

**Challenge:** Evolving regulations (GDPR, AML) result in audit failures and rework.

**Solution:** We created a policy-driven compliance test suite that auto-validates data patterns (PII masking, transaction screening) and generates audit-ready reports, reducing nonconformance findings by 70%.

## Test Automation

**Challenge:** Manual scripting is slow, error-prone, and leads to brittle tests.

**Solution:** We implemented a modular BDD framework (Selenium/Playwright + Cucumber) with an AI-powered locator generator, reducing script development time by 50% and eliminating 80% of locator-related failures.

## Test Assessment for Maturity

**Challenge:** Lack of visibility into QA processes impedes continuous improvement.

**Solution:** We conducted a CMMI-based maturity assessment, defining key QA metrics (coverage, defect leakage, automation ROI) and delivered a roadmap that elevated the client’s QA maturity from Level 2 to Level 4 in under six months.

![Performance Engineering Icon](data:image/svg+xml;base64...)

## Performance Engineering

**Challenge:** Performance issues emerge late due to absence of early load testing.

**Solution:** We integrated JMeter-based performance tests into CI/CD pipelines and leveraged Kubernetes-driven, geo-distributed load generation, identifying and resolving a 60% serialization bottleneck before production rollout.

## Frequently Asked Qestions

[What is BFSI software testing at Qeagle?](#collapse-14514dc69eeb0729af72)

At **Qeagle**, BFSI (Banking, Financial Services, and Insurance) software testing refers to a comprehensive process of validating software systems used in financial institutions. We ensure these applications are **secure, compliant, scalable, and reliable**, with special attention to industry-specific regulatory requirements.

[Why is BFSI software testing critical for financial institutions?](#collapse-35c8fe469eeb0729af72)

BFSI systems manage sensitive transactions and personal data. Any **failure or vulnerability** can lead to **massive financial losses**, **regulatory penalties**, or **reputational damage**. Qeagle’s rigorous testing services reduce such risks and support **regulatory compliance** (e.g., RBI, SEBI, IRDAI).

[What types of testing does Qeagle offer for BFSI applications?](#collapse-c29457c69eeb0729af72)

Qeagle offers a wide range of testing services, including:

* **Functional Testing** – to validate business workflows
* **Security Testing** – to detect vulnerabilities
* **Performance Testing** – to simulate high-volume transactions
* **API Testing** – for integrations with third-party systems
* **Compliance Testing** – to meet regulatory standards
* **Data Integrity Testing** – to ensure accuracy of financial records

[How does Qeagle ensure data security during BFSI testing?](#collapse-f0f54c169eeb0729af72)

Qeagle follows **OWASP guidelines**, conducts **penetration tests**, and employs **data masking** techniques. We use **secured test environments**, comply with **ISO/IEC standards**, and ensure **role-based access control (RBAC)** for all sensitive testing operations.

[Can Qeagle handle core banking and insurance system testing?](#collapse-d9162c969eeb0729af72)

Yes, Qeagle has extensive experience testing **core banking systems (CBS)**, **insurance policy admin systems**, **loan origination platforms**, **mobile banking apps**, and **wealth management tools**. Our domain-specific test accelerators ensure faster go-to-market.

[How does Qeagle help BFSI clients with regulatory compliance?](#collapse-1a9683e69eeb0729af72)

Qeagle’s testing services align with:

* **PCI-DSS** for payment data security
* **GDPR** for data protection
* **SOX** compliance
* **RBI guidelines** for banking software
  We simulate compliance scenarios and test against **audit-ready checklists**.
