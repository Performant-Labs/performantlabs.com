## When Should You Invest in a QA Maturity Assessment?

* High defect leakage into production
* Low test coverage across business-critical modules
* Flaky automation or delayed regression cycles
* Lack of visibility into QA metrics and outcomes
* Testing not integrated into CI/CD workflows
* Team scaling without structured QA processes

## Frequently Asked Qestions

[What does a QA maturity assessment involve?](#collapse-14514dc69eeb06721c04)

It includes interviews, artifact reviews, tool analysis, metrics evaluation, and stakeholder alignment — resulting in a maturity score and roadmap.

[How long does the assessment take?](#collapse-35c8fe469eeb06721c04)

Depending on your team size and scope, assessments typically take 2 to 4 weeks.

[What deliverables do we receive?](#collapse-c29457c69eeb06721c04)

A detailed report with maturity level analysis, key findings, prioritized recommendations, and a step-by-step improvement roadmap.

[Do you follow a specific maturity model?](#collapse-bee147b69eeb06721c04)

Yes. We use industry-standard models like TMMi, ISO 29119, and adapt to Agile and DevOps contexts as needed.

[Can Qeagle help implement the recommended roadmap?](#collapse-232aae369eeb06721c04)

Absolutely. We offer coaching, framework setup, automation support, and governance models to support post-assessment transformation.
