Mobile apps must work across fragmented ecosystems. Qeagle ensures functional accuracy, responsive UI, and smooth integrations—regardless of screen size, OS version, or network speed.

## When Should You Invest in Mobile Testing?

* Frequent app updates across Android & iOS
* Apps integrated with APIs, sensors, payments, or cameras
* Issues reported from specific devices or OS versions
* Requirement to test across 50+ device variants
* Regulatory and app store compliance needs

## Frequently Asked Qestions

[How do you ensure my app works across all devices?](#collapse-14514dc69eeb04d2dba5)

We test across real devices, emulators, and cloud-based labs like BrowserStack and Sauce Labs—covering different OS versions, screen sizes, and brands to catch edge-case issues early.

[Can you automate mobile testing for both Android and iOS?](#collapse-35c8fe469eeb04d2dba5)

Absolutely. We use Appium for cross-platform automation and Espresso/XCUITest for platform-specific cases—plugged into your CI/CD tools for seamless test orchestration.

[Why should I choose Qeagle for mobile QA?](#collapse-c29457c69eeb04d2dba5)

We combine test strategy, device infrastructure, automation, and analytics to help you ship faster with fewer bugs. Our domain-aligned approach ensures your mobile experience is as strong as your backend systems.
