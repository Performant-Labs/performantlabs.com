## Key Offerings

Qeagle provides expert software testing and quality engineering services, leveraging innovative methodologies including AI-driven test automation, comprehensive DevOps solutions, robotic process automation, and strategic QA maturity assessments. Our solutions accelerate product releases, enhance software quality, and deliver measurable impacts for elite enterprises across various sectors.

## An Overview of Our Core Services and Tailored Solutions

[### Test Automation

We develop scalable automation using Selenium, Playwright, and RestAssured to test web, mobile, and APIs, minimise manual effort, boost test coverage, reduce defects by 70%, and increase release confidence by up to 95%.](https://www.qeagle.com/test-automation/)

[### DevOps Automation

We implement CI/CD, Docker, Kubernetes, AWS, and Azure to streamline delivery. Our DevOps practice boosts deployment speed, cuts failure rates, and enables faster feedback with 20x test scaling, giving clients a strong competitive edge.](https://www.qeagle.com/devops-automation/)

[### Robotic Process Automation

We automate web, desktop, mainframe, and API tasks using UiPath and BluePrism, cutting manual effort, errors, and costs. Our RPA delivers faster fraud reviews and quicker invoice processing, ensuring compliance and operational excellence.](https://www.qeagle.com/robotic-process-automation/)

[### Performance Engineering

We use JMeter, Gatling, and Prometheus to go beyond load testing, identifying system limits and bottlenecks. Our performance engineering boosts stability, minimises downtime, and enhances user experience under high-load conditions.](https://www.qeagle.com/performance-engineering/)

[### QA Maturity Assessments

We assess QA processes using industry benchmarks to identify gaps and boost efficiency. Our insights promote AI-driven testing, better test data management, and continuous quality, giving clients clear visibility and a roadmap for QA excellence.](https://www.qeagle.com/qa-maturity-assessments/)
