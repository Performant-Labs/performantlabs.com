What I see different

Plain-English summary of every visible delta between the live homepage and the canonical preview at the three breakpoints. Items in scope for 8.2 drive the verdict; items out of scope are noted for future sub-cycles.

In scope for sub-cycle 8.2

  • Hero H1 at 768 (the original critical bug): live now wraps "Ship Drupal releases / with confidence." cleanly within the viewport. No horizontal scroll. FIX VERIFIED.
  • Hero padding-inline at 768/1280: live hero content now starts flush left of the visible container at all viewports, matching the preview's zero inline padding. FIX VERIFIED.
  • Logo grid at 768: live now displays the six logos in two rows of three (CBS Interactive / DocuSign / Orange; Renesas / Robert Half / Tesla) instead of overflowing the viewport horizontally. FIX VERIFIED.
  • Logo grid at 1280: live still uses single-row layout (six logos on one line). No regression.

Out of scope for sub-cycle 8.2 (deferred to other sub-cycles)

  • Hero whitespace — live has roughly 30 px more breathing room above the kicker than the preview at 1280, and ~125 px more at 768. Hero content shifts down on live. This vertical offset is what drives the ~7–13% pixel delta on the hero crops. Slated for sub-cycle 8.4.
  • Header / nav — live shows full nav (Services, How we do it, Articles, Open source projects, About us, Contact us) and a solid teal "Book a testing review" button on the right. Preview shows logo on the left, lighter nav, and a teal "Call today" pill. Slated for sub-cycle 8.1.
  • Hero CTA button styling — live uses solid teal primary + outlined secondary in deep blue. Preview uses two teal-tinted pill buttons (filled teal + outlined teal). Token / button-component work, not layout. Slated for a button-polish sub-cycle.
  • Logo-grid logo treatment — live shows bitmap vendor logos (CBS Interactive icon, DocuSign script, Orange box, Tesla wordmark, etc.). Preview shows uniform text-only logo names. Slated for sub-cycle 8.3 (text-fallback strategy).
  • Mobile (375) — same hero-content vertical offset and same logo treatment differences as above. Mobile hero now wraps to 4 lines on live vs 4 lines on preview (matching). No horizontal scroll on either. Mobile-specific deferred items mirror the desktop ones.
  • Below-the-fold sections — feature cards, footer-CTA, FAQ, footer all show DELTA against preview because their respective sub-cycles (8.4, 8.5, 8.6) have not run. Whole-page diff metric reflects this; do not interpret it as 8.2 regression.
Why the hero crops show 7–13% delta despite passing the visual check. ImageMagick's compare -metric AE compares pixel-by-pixel at the same coordinates. Because live has ~30 px more whitespace above the hero kicker than preview, every glyph in the live hero is offset downward by that amount relative to the same glyph in the preview hero. Each offset glyph reads as "different" on a per-pixel basis even though the rendered text, line-wrap, and layout are visually identical. Drag the wipe-slider comparators below — the hero content is the same; the vertical position differs. That position fix is sub-cycle 8.4's job. For sub-cycle 8.2, the binding evidence is (1) no horizontal scroll at any viewport, (2) H1 wraps cleanly within 768, and (3) logo grid wraps to multiple rows at 768. All three confirmed.
