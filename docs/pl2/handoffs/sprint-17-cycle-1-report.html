What I see different — plain-English summary

  • Hero (all viewports): H1 sits ~180 px lower on live than preview (Drupal hero padding cascade). At 1280, live's H1 "What we maintain in the open" fits on one line; preview wraps it to two lines ("What we maintain" / "in the open"). Preview's hero `__inner` container is narrower (920 px vs live's ~959 px).
  • Heading color: Live H1 + every card H3 renders in espresso #1F1A14; preview uses the slightly lighter #2A2520. Both are legitimate brief tokens, but the live darker token has been the Sprint 13–16 sitewide baseline.
  • Card structure (PC-11): On live, the entire card title is the link (you can click the title to open the project page). On preview, the title is plain text and there's a separate "Read the docs →" footer link in each card. Per PC-11 pre-commitment, live is canonical; preview will be updated to title-as-link with no separate footer link.
  • Other modules (§D): Live has a Payment Stripe card under the "Other modules we maintain" heading; preview's section is empty. Preview-doc needs to add this card.
  • Mobile 375 / §C "Community contributions": H2 wraps to 2 lines with last line = "contributions" (single-word orphan) on BOTH live and preview. Needs a `` between "Community" and "contributions" or a 5-word copy edit.
  • Closing CTA: Live's "Drop us a line" button is a taller outline pill (56 px) with a chevron-circle suffix icon; preview's is a shorter ghost pill (45 px). Sitewide carry (F-NEW-4), not /open-source-projects-specific.
  • Hero kicker / subhead / body P: Sub-pixel line-height drifts (body-lg sitewide carry). Not blocking.
