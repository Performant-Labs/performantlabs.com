# Performant Labs — Repositioning Framework v1

*Phase 1 deliverable of the [repositioning runbook](./repositioning-runbook.md). Approved 2026-04-21 by André Angelantoni.*

**Tagline (working):** *The Drupal AI Testing Experts*

---

## Ideal Customer Profile

Common-denominator hero target:

> Organizations running business-critical Drupal sites who need release confidence without standing up their own testing org.

This deliberately spans four buyer segments: agency COOs, enterprise engineering directors, agency principals, and government/public-sector procurement. Secondary scope: "All of the above" — copy should not foreclose any of them.

---

## Positioning Statement

> For Drupal teams who need confidence that every release ships clean, Performant Labs is the Drupal testing partner that built the tools the ecosystem depends on (ATK, Testor), extends them with AI-era workflows, and — when you need it — embeds testing experts alongside your team.

Three load-bearing elements, in order: **tools · AI workflows · optional people.** Copy must not drop any of the three, but may re-weight them per audience.

---

## What "AI-era workflows" Concretely Means

**Hero capability:** Autonomous test healing — AI agents that diagnose and fix broken Playwright tests in CI.

**Proof:** Running nightly on performantlabs.com itself (see `.github/workflows/heal-tests-claude.yml`).

Other AI capabilities that are in the toolkit but do **not** anchor the story: AI-assisted test authoring, AI triage of flaky tests, AI-driven visual review. Mention in specific sales contexts; do not put in the hero.

## Competitors We're Positioning Against

| Competitor | Buyer assumption | Our counter |
|---|---|---|
| In-house DIY (client's own engineers writing Playwright/Cypress) | "We'll just have our team do it." | Hiring and retaining a testing engineer costs more and ships slower than our tools + team hybrid. |
| General-purpose AI test tools (Mabl, Testim, Autify, etc.) | "We'll point one of these at our Drupal site." | They don't know what Drupal is — no Layout Builder awareness, no Paragraphs, no Twig selectors. |
| QA staffing firms / generic testing consultancies | "Let's get a testing vendor in." | Generic testers learn Drupal on the client's dime; we've been doing this for years. |

**Not competitors — treat as referral partners:** Lullabot, FFW, Acquia Professional Services, and other Drupal build shops. Copy should be explicitly agency-friendly.

## Tone of Voice

**One word: *Pragmatic.***

Test: would a jaded engineer roll their eyes at this sentence? If yes, cut it.

- No hype, no hand-waving. Specifics over adjectives.
- "Here's what AI does. Here's what it doesn't."
- Plain words. Short sentences. No jargon sandbags.

---

## We Are NOT

- A hype-driven "AI solves everything" vendor.
- A Drupal build shop or site-builder agency.
- A body shop or cheap offshore QA provider.

(Note: "Drupal-only" is *not* on this list — we lead with Drupal but do not foreclose non-Drupal engagements.)
