Performant Labs contributes code, helps organize events and gives presentations to the Drupal open source community — not just because we believe in giving back (which we do). Deeply participating in Drupal gives us these tangible benefits:

* We expand our expertise
* We stay current with a fast-moving industry
* We create functionality needed by our clients
* We establish important relationships with other experts

Here are some projects we have initiated to enhance the Drupal experience.

![Drupal Quality Initiative Logo](https://pl-performantlabs.com.2.ddev.site/sites/default/files/migration/open-source-projects/dqi-logo.png?alternateWidths=https%3A//pl-performantlabs.com.2.ddev.site/sites/default/files/styles/canvas_parametrized_width--%7Bwidth%7D/public/migration/open-source-projects/dqi-logo.png.avif%3Fitok%3DIapjFtAD)

[### Drupal Quality Initiative](https://www.drupal.org/project/dqi)

**Building the community capacity for quality.**

This community initiative was born when we were faced with the question of when to move a project from alpha to beta. Andre presented the project at the Bay Area Drupal Camp. Visit the project page on Drupal.org and watch the presentation to learn our thoughts on what it takes to create quality software.

![Campaign Kit Logo](https://pl-performantlabs.com.2.ddev.site/sites/default/files/migration/open-source-projects/ck-logo.png?alternateWidths=https%3A//pl-performantlabs.com.2.ddev.site/sites/default/files/styles/canvas_parametrized_width--%7Bwidth%7D/public/migration/open-source-projects/ck-logo.png.avif%3Fitok%3DlITdswuD)

[### Campaign Kit](https://www.drupal.org/project/campaign_kit)

**A flexible donation system.**

Campaign Kit allows non-profits to create standalone and peer-to-peer donation campaigns on their site. Currently in beta. Visit the project page on Drupal.org, which has a video tour.

![Layout Builder Kit Logo](https://pl-performantlabs.com.2.ddev.site/sites/default/files/migration/open-source-projects/lbk-logo_1.png?alternateWidths=https%3A//pl-performantlabs.com.2.ddev.site/sites/default/files/styles/canvas_parametrized_width--%7Bwidth%7D/public/migration/open-source-projects/lbk-logo_1.png.avif%3Fitok%3D61gJ_koT)

[### Layout Builder Kit](https://www.drupal.org/project/layout_builder_kit)

**Ready-to-use Layout Builder Components.**

With Layout Builder quickly becoming the preferred way of creating flexible page layouts, the community needs components to build pages quickly. Currently in Beta. Visit the project page on Drupal.org.
